Lebron James

The King

Timeline of greatest achievements:

  • 2003 June 26-The Cleveland Cavaliers select James with the first pick in the 2003 NBA Draft.
  • 2004 April 21 — James wins the Rookie of the Year Award after posting averages of 20.9 points, 5.9 assists and 5.5 rebounds per game.
  • 2012 June 21 — The Heat win their second NBA title and first for James by defeating the Oklahoma City Thunder in five games.
  • 2013 June 20 — James wins his second NBA championship as the Heat beat the Spurs in a seven-game series.
  • 2016 June 19 — After leading Cleveland back from 3-1 hole, James gives the Cavaliers their first NBA title by outlasting the Golden State Warriors in seven games.
  • 2020 October 11 — After a heated battle with the Miami heat with his new team the Los Angeles Lakers, Lebron pulls through to win his 4th NBA championship title in honor of his late friend Kobe Bryant.
